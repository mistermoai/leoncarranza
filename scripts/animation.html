Animations

These are samples of my animation work. Software I use includes Adobe After Effects and Adobe Animate. For prototyping or doing quick animation sketches, I use Procreate's Animation feature.

College Savings Video

An animation I did for an entry into a scholarship competition. The topic was how to save money in college, and I made this the summer before my freshman year in Cornell.

eCornell - Women in Leadership certificate Word Cloud

I was tasked to make a Word Cloud-style animation for eCornell's Women in Leadership certificate. I was given a list of microaggression-phrases to consider and use my work.

The Playground

Friend and musician Takahito Mori pitched the idea of PromptQ to me—a collective of visual and musical artists could come together and collaborate. One could "prompt" the other to make work that complements their own. Here, I made a simple pixel swing animation to accompany Taka's music.
